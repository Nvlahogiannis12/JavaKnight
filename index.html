Rules:

Java Knight is a turn-based combat game where you face 3 rounds of enemies, including monsters and bosses.

Combat: Attack success depends on luck. Damage ranges from 10-30, with higher damage needing more luck.

Shielding: Shields block all damage and deal 5 damage but can only be used every other turn.

Upgrades: After each round, upgrade your stats to prepare for the next battle. Choose wisely!
